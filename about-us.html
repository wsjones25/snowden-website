About us

Welcome to Mulanje Adventures. I know we're biased but we think Mulanje is the best place - there is so much to see and do.

Oz Snow has been committed to offering quality snow trips at a reasonable price to skiers and boarders since 1999. We can offer flexible packages to all individuals and groups including Corporate, University, School and Community groups at a competitive price. Oz Snow designs their tours to be fun filled, good value for money and will remove the stress from organising your snow holiday.

Oz Snow is an all in one accredited tour operator, travel agent, hotel and lodge owner. This enables us to have greater control and organisation of our tours as well as keeping our prices down with better facilities and services to catering to our customers needs. Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced skier or boarder come along and experience the snow scene for a fun filled trip with Oz Snow.
