About us

Welcome to Mulanje Adventures. I know we're biased but we think Mulanje is the best place - there is so much to see and do.

Mulanje Adventures is a dedicated and locally based guiding service for activities in Mulanje. We live and work at the foot of this beautiful mountain and absolutely love supporting others to enjoy its many paths, ridges and summits as well as the various communities that surround the mountain. We know the many characters of this majestic mountain: its quiet and popular paths, its many stories...

We provide bespoke guided days, family walks, guided educational and school groups. We are keen to work within and support our local community, mountain conservation campaigns and doing everything we can to ensure your mountain memories are made to last. On your tour, you will be guided by locally based, experienced guides (and porters) who know the mountain inside out and will do all they can to make your experience a memorable one. We hope you love this mountain as much as we do.

Furahia - Enjoy,

Snowden
(Owner of Mulanje Adventures)
